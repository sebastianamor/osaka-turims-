Practical Tips

💡 Tip 1

Always tap in AND out with your IC card — failing to tap out charges the maximum fare.

💡 Tip 2

Taxis are reliable but expensive. Avoid during rush hour — use the subway instead.

💡 Tip 3

KIX to Namba: Nankai Rapi:t express — 38 minutes, ¥1,430.

💡 Tip 4

Rent a bicycle — Osaka is very cycle-friendly with dedicated paths near the river.
